Apple Pie

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the crust by mixing flour, butter, and water.
  3. Peel and slice the apples, and mix them with sugar and cinnamon.
  4. Roll out the crust and place it in a pie dish.
  5. Fill the crust with the apple mixture.
  6. Cover with the top crust and seal the edges.
  7. Bake for 50-60 minutes until golden brown.
