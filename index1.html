French Onion Soup

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat butter in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and cook until caramelized, about 25 minutes.
  3. Add garlic, thyme, and flour, then stir to combine.
  4. Pour in beef broth and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  6. Ladle soup into bowls, top with bread and cheese, and broil until melted.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
